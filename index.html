Fressh Garden Salad

Prep time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Mixed greens
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber

    Instructions:

  1. Wash all vegetables thoroughly
  2. Chop vegetables into bite-sized pieces
  3. Mix greens in a large bowl
  4. Add dressing and toss gently
  5. Serve immediately

Classic Pasta Primavera

Prep time: 30 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Pasta (penne or spaghetti)
  • Bell peppers
  • Zucchini

instructions:

  1. Boil pasta according to package directions
  2. Saute vegetables in olive oil
  3. Add garlic and cook until fragrant
  4. Combine pasta with vegetables
  5. Top with parmesan and serve hot

Healthy Breakfast Bowl

Prep time: 15 minutes

Ingredients:

  • Greek yogurt
  • Fresh berries
  • Granola

Instructions:

  1. Add yogurt to a bowl
  2. Top with fresh berries
  3. Sprinkle granola on top
  4. Drizzle with honey
  5. Add chia seeds and enjoy
